> Robot Arm


_A two-link robot arm written in C++ and SDL2.


_Langragian equations for a double pendulum define the dynamics of the system.


_Inverse kinematics are calculated to retrieve the joint angle necessary for the end-effector to reach the desired target defined by the mouse cursor.


_A non-linear controller defines the behaviour of the two motors at the joints, which can apply a limited torque to the pendulum.


_Click into the window to get access to additional functionalities.

__Press 'c' to toggle the controller.

__Press 't' to toggle the trace.
